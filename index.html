What is good design?

In my opinion, a good design is well-communicated, clean and clear, recognizable, cohesive/coordinated, and intentional. I am partial to very clean-cut, sleek designs that clearly communicates the desired information, as well as strong color schemes. If a design isn't visually appealing, it loses influence. A good design must be understandable, otherwise it loses its purpose and connection to the audience.
